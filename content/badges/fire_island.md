# Fire Island Badges

Island-wide badges for anyone staying anywhere on Fire Island. These sit between the Kismet-specific badges and the universal badges — they require Fire Island, but not Kismet specifically.

Fire Island has 17 communities. These badges work for all of them.

---

## The Ferry

**Bronze** — Take the ferry to Fire Island for the first time. Stay for at least one night.
**Silver** — Miss the last ferry of the night and figure it out.
**Gold** — Take the ferry so many times that you stop thinking about it — it's just how you get there.

---

## Car-Free

**Bronze** — Spend a full weekend on Fire Island without being in a car once.
**Silver** — Walk or bike to something you would have driven to anywhere else.
**Gold** — Spend 10 or more nights on Fire Island total, all of them car-free.

---

## The Ocean vs. The Bay

**Bronze** — Swim in the ocean on the south side.
**Silver** — Swim in the bay on the north side.
**Gold** — Swim in both on the same day.

---

## The Lighthouse

**Bronze** — Visit the Fire Island Lighthouse.
**Silver** — Climb to the top of the lighthouse.
**Gold** — Watch the sun rise or set from the lighthouse grounds. Be there when the light catches.

---

## The Communities

**Bronze** — Visit a Fire Island community other than the one you're staying in.
**Silver** — Visit 5 different Fire Island communities.
**Gold** — Visit all 17 Fire Island communities over any number of trips.

---

## National Seashore

**Bronze** — Walk through the Sunken Forest in Sailors Haven.
**Silver** — Walk the Wilderness area east of Watch Hill — the stretch of undeveloped barrier island with no communities.
**Gold** — Walk from one end of the National Seashore to the other, all on foot.

---

## Wildlife

**Bronze** — See a deer on Fire Island.
**Silver** — See a red fox, a osprey, and a horseshoe crab — all in a single season.
**Gold** — Spot a piping plover nest (look, don't touch) and know what it is.

---

## The Water Taxi

Take the Water Taxi between at least two Fire Island communities. Do not take the same route back.

---

## The Dock

**Bronze** — Fish off a dock anywhere on Fire Island.
**Silver** — Catch something edible off a dock and cook it that night.
**Gold** — Catch dinner for the whole house from the bay or ocean.

---

## Seasonal

**Bronze** — Come to Fire Island in a month other than July or August.
**Silver** — Come in both the opening weekend and the closing weekend of the season.
**Gold** — See Fire Island in the off-season — after the ferries go to reduced schedule, after the communities quiet down. See what it actually is.

---

## The Walk Between Towns

Walk between two Fire Island communities on the beach or the boardwalk without taking a water taxi or ferry. Do it in one go.

---

## The Storm

Experience a named storm, a nor'easter, or a significant weather event at a Fire Island community. Stay for it.

---

## The Night Sky

**Bronze** — Look at the stars from the beach on Fire Island after midnight. No phone, no flashlight. Let your eyes adjust.
**Silver** — Identify at least 5 constellations.
**Gold** — Watch a meteor shower from the beach. Plan for it.

---

## The Sunrise Run

Run on the beach at sunrise. Not the road, not the boardwalk — the beach. Early enough that you have it mostly to yourself.
