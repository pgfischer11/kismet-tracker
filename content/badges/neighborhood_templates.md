# Neighborhood Badge Templates

Plug-and-play badge templates for any neighborhood. Replace [NEIGHBORHOOD] with the location name. These provide coverage where hyper-local knowledge doesn't exist yet.

---

## Food & Drink Exploration

**The Full Block**
Bronze — Eat at 3 different restaurants in [NEIGHBORHOOD] you've never tried.
Silver — Eat at 10 different restaurants in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Eat at 25 different restaurants in [NEIGHBORHOOD]. Become the person people ask.
Use case: Works in any dense urban neighborhood with a real restaurant scene.

**The Local Drink**
Bronze — Find a bar in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and order whatever's on tap.
Silver — Become a regular at a bar in [NEIGHBORHOOD] — they know your name.
Gold — Find a hidden bar, speakeasy, or unmarked door in [NEIGHBORHOOD] that most people don't know about.
Use case: Best for neighborhoods with active nightlife.

**The Market Run**
Bronze — Shop for a week's groceries at a local market or bodega in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Find the best produce shop, butcher, and bakery in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and use all three in one meal.
Gold — Cook a full meal using only ingredients sourced from [NEIGHBORHOOD] businesses.
Use case: Works anywhere with independent food retail.

**The Coffee Circuit**
Bronze — Find a non-chain coffee shop in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and make it your spot for a week.
Silver — Try every independent coffee shop in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Find a coffee shop in [NEIGHBORHOOD] that feels like yours — the staff knows you, you have a usual.
Use case: Urban neighborhoods with independent café culture.

**The Brunch Table**
Bronze — Get brunch at a [NEIGHBORHOOD] spot on a weekend.
Silver — Wait more than 30 minutes for a table somewhere in [NEIGHBORHOOD] because it's worth it.
Gold — Host a brunch at home for at least 6 people, with food sourced from [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Use case: Any neighborhood with a weekend brunch culture.

## Community Involvement

**The Local Meeting**
Bronze — Attend a community board meeting, town hall, or neighborhood association meeting in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Speak at one.
Gold — Show up to 5 in a year.
Use case: Any residential neighborhood with local governance.

**The Cleanup**
Bronze — Participate in a neighborhood cleanup or beautification event in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Organize one.
Gold — Start a recurring one that happens without you having to plan it every time.
Use case: Universal.

**The Local Business**
Bronze — Shop at a locally-owned business in [NEIGHBORHOOD] instead of a chain, when both options exist.
Silver — Do this for a full month — every purchase you can make local, you do.
Gold — Become a regular at a local business in [NEIGHBORHOOD]. They recognize you.
Use case: Universal.

## Local Discovery

**The Hidden Spot**
Bronze — Find something in [NEIGHBORHOOD] that isn't on any list, app, or map — a garden, a view, a shortcut, a mural.
Silver — Show it to someone else.
Gold — Find 5 things like this.
Use case: Universal. Works especially well in dense urban neighborhoods.

**The History**
Bronze — Learn something specific about the history of [NEIGHBORHOOD] — one real fact, not surface level.
Silver — Find a physical landmark in [NEIGHBORHOOD] that most residents don't know exists.
Gold — Give someone a walking tour of [NEIGHBORHOOD] based on things you've found yourself.
Use case: Works best in neighborhoods with interesting or layered histories.

**The Locals' Map**
Bronze — Get a recommendation from someone who actually lives in [NEIGHBORHOOD] (not a tourist, not an app).
Silver — Build a list of 10 recommendations in [NEIGHBORHOOD], all from people not from the internet.
Gold — Give someone visiting [NEIGHBORHOOD] a list of recommendations that surprises them.
Use case: Universal.

**The Annual Event**
Bronze — Attend a recurring annual event specific to [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Attend it two years in a row.
Gold — Miss it and feel genuinely bad about it. You know you've arrived when.
Use case: Neighborhoods with street fairs, parades, festivals, or regular seasonal events.

## Movement & Outdoors

**The Full Walk**
Bronze — Walk every block of the main street(s) in [NEIGHBORHOOD] in a single outing.
Silver — Walk every block in the entire [NEIGHBORHOOD] over any amount of time.
Gold — Do it again in a different season and notice what changed.
Use case: Universal — works in any walkable neighborhood.

**The Park**
Bronze — Find every park or green space in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Spend at least one full afternoon in each.
Gold — Have a park in [NEIGHBORHOOD] you'd call yours. Go back enough times that it earns that.
Use case: Works anywhere with accessible parks.

**The Early Morning**
Bronze — See [NEIGHBORHOOD] before 7am. Walk around. See what it's like when it's empty.
Silver — Do this in every season.
Gold — Find something you love about [NEIGHBORHOOD] that only exists in the early morning.
Use case: Universal — especially good for dense urban areas.

## Culture & Arts

**The Galleries**
Bronze — Go into a gallery in or near [NEIGHBORHOOD] with no particular plan.
Silver — Go to a gallery opening in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Buy a piece of art made by a local artist in or near [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Use case: Neighborhoods with active arts scenes.

**The Performance**
Bronze — See any live performance — music, theater, comedy, spoken word — in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — See 5 live performances in [NEIGHBORHOOD] over any time period.
Gold — Become a regular at one venue in [NEIGHBORHOOD]. Know the people who run it.
Use case: Neighborhoods with live performance venues.

**The Mural**
Bronze — Find a street mural in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and find out who made it.
Silver — Find 5 murals in [NEIGHBORHOOD] and document them.
Gold — Commission or coordinate a piece of public art in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Use case: Urban neighborhoods with street art.

## Sports & Recreation

**The Local Game**
Bronze — Play a sport or join a recreational activity in [NEIGHBORHOOD] with people you don't know.
Silver — Join a recurring league or pickup game in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Become a regular — someone who's missed when you're not there.
Use case: Universal — parks, courts, fields, gyms.

**The Gym or Studio**
Bronze — Try a fitness class, gym, or studio in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Silver — Go back 10 times.
Gold — Be a regular at something in [NEIGHBORHOOD] that you'd genuinely miss if it closed.

## Becoming a Regular / Local Identity

**The Usual**
Bronze — Order "the usual" somewhere in [NEIGHBORHOOD] — and have them know what it means.
Silver — Have a usual at 3 different places in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Be introduced by someone as "a [NEIGHBORHOOD] person." Earn it.
Use case: Universal.

**The Neighbor**
Bronze — Introduce yourself to a neighbor in [NEIGHBORHOOD] you've never spoken to.
Silver — Know the names of at least 5 neighbors in [NEIGHBORHOOD].
Gold — Have a neighbor in [NEIGHBORHOOD] who would call you if something was wrong. Be that for someone too.
Use case: Residential neighborhoods.

**The Years**
Bronze — Live in or spend regular time in [NEIGHBORHOOD] for at least 1 year.
Silver — 3 years.
Gold — 5 years. Watch it change. Miss some things. Stay anyway.
Use case: For people who actually live in a neighborhood, not just visit.

**The Recommendation**
Bronze — Give someone visiting [NEIGHBORHOOD] a single recommendation they wouldn't find on their own.
Silver — Give 3 people recommendations that turn out to be exactly right for them.
Gold — Become the person people ask before visiting [NEIGHBORHOOD]. Be worth asking.
Use case: Universal.
