# Universal Badges

These badges work for anyone, anywhere. They are the soul of the app — the foundation that every location builds on top of. No location knowledge required. You know when you've done it.

---

## Movement & Fitness

**Bronze** — Go for a run somewhere you've never run before.
**Silver** — Complete a hike of at least 5 miles in a single day.
**Gold** — Train for and finish a race of any distance (5k counts).

---

## Food & Cooking

**Bronze** — Cook a meal completely from scratch (min. 3 items including a protein).
**Silver** — Cook the same dish 5 times until you've actually got it right.
**Gold** — Host a dinner for at least 6 people and cook every dish yourself.

---

## Community & Neighbors

**Bronze** — Attend something in your neighborhood you've never been to before.
**Silver** — Introduce yourself to a neighbor you've never spoken to.
**Gold** — Host a block party, potluck, or neighborhood gathering at your place.

---

## Nature & Outdoors

**Bronze** — Watch a full sunrise or sunset without looking at your phone.
**Silver** — Identify 5 birds, plants, or trees in your area by name.
**Gold** — Spend a night outside — camping, a backyard, a roof, anywhere.

---

## Social & Friendship

**Bronze** — Call someone you haven't spoken to in over a year.
**Silver** — Plan and execute a trip with at least 3 friends — flights, accommodation, the whole thing.
**Gold** — Show up for a friend during a genuinely hard moment — not a text, actually show up.

---

## Creativity

**Bronze** — Finish something you made: a song, a drawing, a piece of writing, a meal, anything.
**Silver** — Share something you made with people outside your immediate circle.
**Gold** — Start and finish a creative project that takes more than a month to complete.

---

## Learning & Curiosity

**Bronze** — Read a non-fiction book cover to cover.
**Silver** — Learn a skill that requires at least 10 hours of practice to become passable at.
**Gold** — Teach someone else something you learned from scratch in the last 2 years.

---

## Wellness & Rest

**Bronze** — Take a full day off — no work, no to-do list, no catching up.
**Silver** — Get 8+ hours of sleep every night for a full week.
**Gold** — Go two weeks without drinking alcohol. No special occasion required.

---

## Adventure & Exploration

**Bronze** — Go somewhere within 2 hours of home that you've always meant to visit and never have.
**Silver** — Take a solo trip for at least one night — just you.
**Gold** — Go somewhere you've never been that requires a passport or a plane.

---

## Sports & Competition

**Bronze** — Play a sport with strangers.
**Silver** — Compete in something with an actual scoreboard — tournament, league, race, anything.
**Gold** — Win something. A game, a tournament, a bet, doesn't matter. Win it fair.

---

## Giving Back

**Bronze** — Volunteer for a single shift at any organization.
**Silver** — Donate something meaningful — time, money, or skills — on a recurring basis for at least 3 months.
**Gold** — Organize something for others: a fundraiser, a cleanup, a drive. You do the logistics, not just show up.

---

## Home & Sanctuary

**Bronze** — Host people at your place — dinner, drinks, anything with more than 2 guests.
**Silver** — Spend a full weekend making your home actually how you want it — furniture, art, whatever it takes.
**Gold** — Live somewhere long enough that you become a regular somewhere nearby: a coffee shop, a bar, a diner.

---

## Work & Ambition

**Bronze** — Finish a project you've been putting off for more than 3 months.
**Silver** — Start something new — a side project, a business, a creative venture — and spend at least 40 hours on it.
**Gold** — Ship something publicly: a product, a piece of writing, a service. Something real people can find.

---

## Seasons & Time

**Bronze** — Do something to mark the change of a season — a meal, a walk, a tradition. Do it intentionally.
**Silver** — Do the same thing in the same place, at the same time of year, two years in a row.
**Gold** — Build a real annual tradition: something your people look forward to and plan around, three years running.

---

## Wildcard / Unexpected

**Bronze** — Strike up a real conversation with a stranger — not small talk, actually learn something about them.
**Silver** — Say yes to something you would normally say no to. See what happens.
**Gold** — Do something that genuinely scares you. Not skydiving-for-the-thrill scared — actually scared. Then do it anyway.
